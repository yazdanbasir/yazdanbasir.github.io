I worked as Lafayette Men's Soccer's first-ever data engineer and analyst. I developed a data processing pipeline that scraped, consolidated, and cleaned the team's performance metrics from Wyscout and supplied it to a custom-built dashboard, where the various statistics could be filtered and visualized according to the needs of the coaches/team to inform training strategy and opponent analysis.

A web application to streamline the organization of one of Lafayette College's major annual events, Food Tasting (hosted by the International Students' Association). This project integrates Giant's inventory and automates, cleans up, and consolidates the process of ingredient submission, grocery shopping, and resource allocation for the big day!

My full-stack application for privately managing my notes, finances, to-do lists, project progress, collections of items, and article reading (IN PROGRESS).

Even though I've been watching soccer/football for more than a decade, I still stumble across players that I had no idea played with each other at some point. Therefore, I developed this tool to be able to search for a particular player and visualize when and how long they overlapped with all teammates across their career.

I've always been curious to see what my sleeping patterns can tell me about my body and my habits. I've been tracking the data with a FitBit and built a dashboard to feed the metrics into where I can visualize, filter, and draw insights from in order optimize my routine/well-being.
