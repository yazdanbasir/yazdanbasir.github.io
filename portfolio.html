Lafayette Men's Soccer

I worked as the first-ever data engineer and analyst for Lafayette's Men's Soccer team. I developed a data processing pipeline that scraped, consolidated, and cleaned the team's performance metrics from Wyscout and supplied it to a custom-built dashboard, where the various statistics could be filtered and visualized according to the needs of the coaches/team to inform training strategy and opponent analysis.

Link to the tool. (Data anonymized for privacy)

NightSight

For as long as I can remember, I've had trouble falling asleep. So, I've always been curious to see what my sleeping patterns can tell me about my body and my habits. I've been tracking the data with a FitBit and built a dashboard to feed the metrics into where I can visualize, filter, and draw insights from in order optimize my routine/well-being.
