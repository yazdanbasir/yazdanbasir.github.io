Projects in development

Working with San Diego FC on data engineering and analytics projects to support the team's performance and operations.

I worked as Lafayette Men's Soccer's first-ever data engineer and analyst. I developed a data processing pipeline that scraped, consolidated, and cleaned the team's performance metrics from Wyscout and supplied it to a custom-built dashboard, where the various statistics could be filtered and visualized according to the needs of the coaches/team to inform training strategy and opponent analysis.

A web application to streamline the organization of one of Lafayette College's major annual events, Food Tasting (hosted by the International Students' Association). This project integrates Giant's inventory and automates, cleans up, and consolidates the process of ingredient submission, grocery shopping, and resource allocation for the big day!

A comprehensive project management and organization system designed to help manage complex workflows and track progress across multiple initiatives.

PlayerNet: Even though I've been watching soccer/football for more than a decade, I still stumble across players that I had no idea played with each other at some point. Therefore, I developed this tool to be able to search for a particular player and visualize when and how long they overlapped with all teammates across their career.
